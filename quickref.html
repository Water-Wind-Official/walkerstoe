Quick Reference

Terms and concise definitions used throughout Walker's TOE.

Information Substrate
The layer or medium through which informational relationships manifest and propagate.
Integration-Differentiation
A balance measure: systems that are both integrated and differentiated support rich dynamics.
Emergent Regularity
Patterns or laws that appear at macroscopic scales from micro-level interactions.
Phenomenal Continuity
The subjective persistence and flow of experience over time.
